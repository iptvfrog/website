IPTV PRODUCTS - INTERNET PROTOCOL TELEVISION

Blue Frog

This is IPTV Frog’s original server, offering over 2500 live TV channels from around the world. Popular premium channels, sports packages, kids programming, world news channels and adult content are all included, many in HD. Along with all this, an extensive movie and TV show video on demand section is also included. The blue server also features a PVR service on many channels, allowing playback of live TV channels up to 7 days after they air. All of this combines to create an unbelievable entertainment experience. Try it today for as low as $9 per month!

Yellow Frog

This server is new to IPTV Frog and was added to give our customers choices. Although the yellow server has much of the same content, there are some noticeable differences. This server has many backup channels for popular premium channels, allowing a convenient substitute if a channel is experiencing a temporary problem. This server also has an expanded sports section. If you are a new customer to IPTV Frog, we suggest that you purchase the 1 month combo-server package and try them both for 1 month. After that you can select your favorite or continue your subscription with both servers.
