DOWNLOADING STB EMULATOR

  1. Open Kodi.
  2. Scroll to Programs on the Kodi menu bar.
  3. Open the APK Downloader.
  4. Select Android.
  5. Go to JayTech Apps.
  6. Select the latest version of the STB Emulator.
  7. Select Download.
  8. When the download is complete, select Install.
  9. When the installation is complete, select Done.
  10. Exit Kodi and return to your Android box's home screen.

Don't have the APK Downloader or Kodi?
No worries! The STB Emulator can be downloaded from the Google Play store.

CONFIGURING STB EMULATOR

  1. Open the STB Emulator.
  2. After it opens, press the Configure Application button OR press the menu key on your remote (looks like a list) and select Settings from the drop down menu that appears.
  3. From the sidebar, select Profiles.
  4. If there are no profiles listed on your program, press the Add Profile button. If there is a profile listed, select it.
  5. Now go into the Portal Settings.
  6. From the portal settings, select the Portal URL.
  7. Press the return key on your remote to hide the onscreen keyboard.
  8. Press and hold the right arrow key on your remote to move the text cursor to the end of the URL.
  9. Press the OK button on your remote to bring back the onscreen keyboard.
  10. Erase the existing URL by pressing and holding the backspace button on the onscreen keyboard on your remote.
  11. Now type http://ez4u.pw into the text box.
  12. Press the return key on the remote to hide the onscreen keyboard.
  13. Press Ok on the text box.
  14. Press the return key on the remote to return to the profile settings screen.
  15. Now select the STB Configuration settings.
  16. Open the MAC-address setting.
  17. Press the return key on your remote to hide the onscreen keyboard.
  18. Press and hold the right arrow key on your remote to move the text cursor to the end of the MAC address.
  19. Press the OK button on your remote to bring back the onscreen keyboard.
  20. Erase the existing MAC address by pressing and holding the backspace button on the onscreen keyboard on your remote.
  21. Now type [Your MAC Address] into the text box.
  22. Press the return key on the remote to hide the onscreen keyboard.
  23. Press Ok on the text box.
  24. Repeatedly press the return key on the remote until you return to the STB Emulator main menu.
  25. Press the menu key on your remote.
  26. Select Exit from the drop-down menu to close the STB Emulator.
  27. Reopen the STB Emulator and your TV menu should now display.

NOTE: If you're account has not been activated yet, you will be asked for login information. This is normal and cannot be bypassed until your account has been activated on our end. This can take up to two business days, after which point your IPTV will automatically start working.
